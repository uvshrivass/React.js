Making dreams come true.

20-02-2019

Believe it or not, dreams are an integral part of our lives. Whether small or big, we all dream in some capacity, it's our inherent tendency that's impossible to diminish. While we all share this bond of dreaming but not many people have the courage to uproot their lives to follow their dreams. I am not one of them and neither are my fellow bootcampers at The Hacking School - India's First Coding Bootcamp who come from around the country and some even other parts of the world.

Eyeing on a common goal of becoming a full stack web developer and making our lives purposeful, we started the bootcamp with some inspirational sessions from Meraj Faheem, CEO, The Hacking School and our instructor Prashanth Teja, CTO, Cedura. Both insisted on the idea of chasing dreams, and advised us to build fast and plenty which literally filled our hearts with immense motivation.

To some going for ambitions seems a far-fetched idea. It sure is, but with enough motivation, support and a constructive environment anything is attainable, and that's what The Hacking School is providing us, a platform to follow our dreams and passion of becoming a better us and building something meaningful. Very true that the journey has just started and there is still a long way to go but as legendary Walter Disney said, "All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them", we hope to keep our heads up and hearts open till we reach our destination.

To know more about our pursuit, stay tuned!
